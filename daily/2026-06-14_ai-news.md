### 3. [新機能] AWS、ElastiCache for Valkeyに耐久性ストレージオプションを導入

出典: [InfoQ AI/ML](https://www.infoq.com/news/2026/06/elasticache-valkey-durability/?utm_campaign=infoq_content&utm_source=infoq&utm_medium=feed&utm_term=AI%2C+ML+%26+Data+Engineering)
